About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Fox Valley Technical College pursuing a degree in Software Development. I am passionate about learning new things and creating apps that empower people.

I originally started learning HTML in highschool, but left it behind when I joined the Army. I have held a latent interest in computer science since then, and recently made the decision to return to school and restart my coding journey.

My first app was a simple punchclock that I use to log my hours working from home. The practical experience of programming this app helped me to solidify my understanding of OOP and n-tier architecture, and gave me the great satisfaction of solving my own needs through software.

I am currently looking for a summer internship where I can gain real-world experience in a large codebase.

This website is a work in progress, and probably always will be, just like me. As I gain new skills, I will employ them in my projects.
